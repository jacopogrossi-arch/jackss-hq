## Scena A — Macchina da cucire

**Durata: 4 secondi**
**Reference image:** `hf_20260601_102110_f0465350-7e50-4ae7-a797-25773cc811dd.png`
**Uso nel montaggio:** opening 0-4s


```
FORMAT: Fashion editorial scene. Single subject. Tailoring workshop setting. Medium shot, slightly elevated angle, locked camera. Duration: 4 seconds.

SUBJECT: Young male fashion designer. Wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace. Seated at a vintage tailoring machine on a wooden worktable. Head tilted forward, eyes focused on his craft. Concentrated, stoic expression. Not looking at camera.

ENVIRONMENT: Luxury fashion atelier. Tailoring equipment on a wooden worktable. Clothing rack with tailored garments in background. Large window to the left with warm amber light. Moody, atmospheric workspace with golden tones.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic cinematic fashion editorial. Warm amber color grade, deep shadows, golden-brown midtones. Warm key light from upper-left. Shallow depth of field, background soft. Clean and still composition.

LOGIC RULE: Camera completely locked — no movement. Subject's head stays tilted forward toward the work throughout. Motion is only in the arms advancing the textile forward. No sudden gestures. Steady and deliberate pace throughout all 4 seconds.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no camera shake, no camera movement, no smile, no eye contact with camera, no abrupt motion.

ACTION: The fashion designer advances a textile piece forward across the tailoring machine — both arms moving slowly, evenly, and with precision. The machine works steadily. His gaze stays on the work. The atmosphere is that of a skilled craftsman focused entirely on his creation. Slow, controlled, elegant motion throughout.
```

## Scena B — Laptop con AI tools

**Durata: 5 secondi**
**Reference image:** `hf_20260601_103201_37c53fe3-b986-47d3-8fab-8e4724aacf33.png`
**Uso nel montaggio:** mid-section ~5-12s
**Nota:** usa l'animazione per correggere il problema sguardo — il movimento inizia con lui che si gira leggermente verso lo schermo

```
FORMAT: Cinematic actionprom scene. Single subject. Over-the-shoulder 3/4 angle, locked frame. Duration: 5 seconds.

SUBJECT: Young man. Dark wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace with pendant. Seated at a wooden table, body in 3/4 angle away from camera. Face visible in 3/4 profile. At the start of the clip, head turns slightly to orient toward the laptop screen — then settles looking at the screen. One hand moves slowly on the trackpad, scrolling. Expression stoic and concentrated throughout.

ENVIRONMENT: Vintage dark-luxury atelier. Wooden work table. Laptop open in front of subject, screen in dark mode — black background, fabric swatch grid visible. Clothing rack with dark garments in background. Dress form mannequin partially visible. Large window to the left with warm amber light. Warm golden tones throughout.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic editorial fashion film. Color grade: warm amber tones, deep shadows, golden-brown midtones. Dual light: warm window light from left + cool soft glow from laptop screen on the face. Shallow depth of field — subject and screen sharp, background soft. No lens flare.

LOGIC RULE: Camera is locked — no movement. Motion is in the hand scrolling on trackpad and the initial subtle head turn toward the screen. After the head settles facing the screen it does not move again. No spontaneous gestures, no head nod, no looking at camera at any point.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no looking at camera, no smile, no pleasant expression, no camera movement, no handheld shake, no sudden gestures, no bright screen background, no white UI on laptop.

ACTION: Clip opens with subject already in 3/4 position. In the first second, his head turns slightly and naturally toward the laptop screen — settling into focus on the display. For the remaining 4 seconds: one hand moves slowly and deliberately on the trackpad, scrolling through content on the dark-mode screen. Expression stoic and still. The laptop screen shows fabric swatches on a dark background, scrolling slowly as the hand moves. Scene is calm and purposeful.
```

## Scena C — Tessuti

**Durata: 5 secondi**
**Reference image:** `hf_20260601_103443_88080baa-f25e-493c-b33e-648b26c82b5e.png`
**Uso nel montaggio:** mid-section ~12-20s

```
FORMAT: Cinematic action scene. Single subject. Medium shot, slightly lateral angle, locked frame. Duration: 5 seconds.

SUBJECT: Young man. Dark wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace with pendant. Standing at work table, holding a large dark fabric swatch with both hands — raised toward the window light. Head tilted slightly, eyes looking at the fabric throughout. Expression stoic and evaluative, jaw set, mouth flat. Not looking at camera.

ENVIRONMENT: Vintage dark-luxury atelier. Work table with dark fabric swatches spread out below — black, charcoal, deep navy. Metal shelving in background, soft and dark. Large window to the left: warm diffused light raking across the held fabric. Warm amber and golden undertones throughout.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic editorial fashion film. Color grade: warm amber tones, deep shadows, golden-brown midtones. Key light from left window grazing the fabric surface — texture of the material is sharp and detailed. Shallow depth of field — hands and fabric sharp, background soft. No lens flare.

LOGIC RULE: Camera is locked throughout — no movement. All motion is in the hands and fabric only. Subject's gaze remains on the fabric — does not look at camera at any point. Motion is slow and deliberate — no sudden gestures, no jerky movement.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no looking at camera, no smile, no pleasant expression, no camera movement, no handheld shake, no sudden movements, no bright background.

ACTION: Subject holds the dark fabric up toward the window light, rotating it very slowly with both hands to catch the texture in the light — a quarter turn over 3 seconds, examining the material closely. In the final 2 seconds, he lowers the fabric slightly back toward the table in a slow, controlled motion, gaze following the material down. The warm window light rakes across the fabric as it moves, revealing the texture and weight of the cloth. Scene is slow, deliberate, expert.
```
