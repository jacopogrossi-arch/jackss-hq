```

barely perceptible push-in — the embroidery gradually fills more of the frame. 
Warm amber raking light from the left, constant throughout. Thread texture sharpens 
as the camera drifts forward. No shake, no handheld motion. The fabric is completely 
still. Duration: 3–4 seconds. Camera movement only.
```
