## Scena A — Macchina da cucire

**Durata: 4 secondi**
**Reference image:** `hf_20260601_102110_f0465350-7e50-4ae7-a797-25773cc811dd.png`
**Uso nel montaggio:** opening 0-4s

```
FORMAT: Fashion editorial scene. Single subject. Tailoring workshop setting. Medium shot, slightly elevated angle, locked camera. Duration: 4 seconds.

SUBJECT: Young male fashion designer. Wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace. Seated at a vintage tailoring machine on a wooden worktable. Head tilted forward, eyes focused on his craft. Concentrated, stoic expression. Not looking at camera.

ENVIRONMENT: Luxury fashion atelier. Tailoring equipment on a wooden worktable. Clothing rack with tailored garments in background. Large window to the left with warm amber light. Moody, atmospheric workspace with golden tones.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic cinematic fashion editorial. Warm amber color grade, deep shadows, golden-brown midtones. Warm key light from upper-left. Shallow depth of field, background soft. Clean and still composition.

LOGIC RULE: Camera completely locked — no movement. Subject's head stays tilted forward toward the work throughout. Motion is only in the arms advancing the textile forward. No sudden gestures. Steady and deliberate pace throughout all 4 seconds.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no camera shake, no camera movement, no smile, no eye contact with camera, no abrupt motion.

ACTION: The fashion designer advances a textile piece forward across the tailoring machine — both arms moving slowly, evenly, and with precision. The machine works steadily. His gaze stays on the work. The atmosphere is that of a skilled craftsman focused entirely on his creation. Slow, controlled, elegant motion throughout.
```

## Scena B — Laptop con AI tools

**Durata: 5 secondi**
**Reference image:** `hf_20260601_103201_37c53fe3-b986-47d3-8fab-8e4724aacf33.png`
**Uso nel montaggio:** mid-section ~5-12s
**Nota:** usa l'animazione per correggere il problema sguardo — il movimento inizia con lui che si gira leggermente verso lo schermo

```
FORMAT: Cinematic action scene. Single subject. Over-the-shoulder 3/4 angle, locked frame. Duration: 5 seconds.

SUBJECT: Young man. Dark wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace with pendant. Seated at a wooden table, body in 3/4 angle away from camera. Face visible in 3/4 profile. At the start of the clip, head turns slightly to orient toward the laptop screen — then settles looking at the screen. One hand moves slowly on the trackpad, scrolling. Expression stoic and concentrated throughout.

ENVIRONMENT: Vintage dark-luxury atelier. Wooden work table. Laptop open in front of subject, screen in dark mode — black background, fabric swatch grid visible. Clothing rack with dark garments in background. Dress form mannequin partially visible. Large window to the left with warm amber light. Warm golden tones throughout.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic editorial fashion film. Color grade: warm amber tones, deep shadows, golden-brown midtones. Dual light: warm window light from left + cool soft glow from laptop screen on the face. Shallow depth of field — subject and screen sharp, background soft. No lens flare.

LOGIC RULE: Camera is locked — no movement. Motion is in the hand scrolling on trackpad and the initial subtle head turn toward the screen. After the head settles facing the screen it does not move again. No spontaneous gestures, no head nod, no looking at camera at any point.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no looking at camera, no smile, no pleasant expression, no camera movement, no handheld shake, no sudden gestures, no bright screen background, no white UI on laptop.

ACTION: Clip opens with subject already in 3/4 position. In the first second, his head turns slightly and naturally toward the laptop screen — settling into focus on the display. For the remaining 4 seconds: one hand moves slowly and deliberately on the trackpad, scrolling through content on the dark-mode screen. Expression stoic and still. The laptop screen shows fabric swatches on a dark background, scrolling slowly as the hand moves. Scene is calm and purposeful.
```

## Scena C — Tessuti

**Durata: 5 secondi**
**Reference image:** `hf_20260601_103443_88080baa-f25e-493c-b33e-648b26c82b5e.png`
**Uso nel montaggio:** mid-section ~12-20s

```
FORMAT: Cinematic action scene. Single subject. Medium shot, slightly lateral angle, locked frame. Duration: 5 seconds.

SUBJECT: Young man. Dark wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace with pendant. Standing at work table, holding a large dark fabric swatch with both hands — raised toward the window light. Head tilted slightly, eyes looking at the fabric throughout. Expression stoic and evaluative, jaw set, mouth flat. Not looking at camera.

ENVIRONMENT: Vintage dark-luxury atelier. Work table with dark fabric swatches spread out below — black, charcoal, deep navy. Metal shelving in background, soft and dark. Large window to the left: warm diffused light raking across the held fabric. Warm amber and golden undertones throughout.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic editorial fashion film. Color grade: warm amber tones, deep shadows, golden-brown midtones. Key light from left window grazing the fabric surface — texture of the material is sharp and detailed. Shallow depth of field — hands and fabric sharp, background soft. No lens flare.

LOGIC RULE: Camera is locked throughout — no movement. All motion is in the hands and fabric only. Subject's gaze remains on the fabric — does not look at camera at any point. Motion is slow and deliberate — no sudden gestures, no jerky movement.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no looking at camera, no smile, no pleasant expression, no camera movement, no handheld shake, no sudden movements, no bright background.

ACTION: Subject holds the dark fabric up toward the window light, rotating it very slowly with both hands to catch the texture in the light — a quarter turn over 3 seconds, examining the material closely. In the final 2 seconds, he lowers the fabric slightly back toward the table in a slow, controlled motion, gaze following the material down. The warm window light rakes across the fabric as it moves, revealing the texture and weight of the cloth. Scene is slow, deliberate, expert.
```

## Scena D — Mood board brand (multi-shot)

**Durata: 6 secondi (3s + 3s)**
**Reference image Shot 1:** `hf_20260601_115329_0a09b3c3-16ba-41ef-9bd1-326a46890f07.png`
**Uso nel montaggio:** sezione "The name. The look. The content. The clients."

```
FORMAT: Multi-shot cinematic sequence. Two shots. Single subject. Total duration: 6 seconds. Dark-luxury fashion atelier throughout. Fast cut between shots.

---

[SHOT 1 — 3 seconds]
Medium shot, slightly lateral angle from the right side. Locked camera.

SUBJECT: Young man. Dark wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace with pendant. Seated at a wide wooden work table, body angled slightly to the side. One hand holds a pen, writing slowly on an open sketchbook. Mood board spread across the table — handwritten name sketches, dark fabric swatches, editorial references. Head tilted slightly downward, eyes focused on the work. Jaw set, mouth flat. Not looking at camera.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic editorial fashion film. Warm amber color grade, deep shadows, golden-brown midtones. Key light from left window, warm and diffused. Shallow depth of field — subject and table sharp, background soft. Expression stoic throughout — jaw set, mouth flat, zero upward curve.

ACTION: Hand moves slowly across the sketchbook, writing with calm precision. Head stays tilted down. Scene is still and purposeful.

---

[SHOT 2 — 3 seconds]
Cut to close-up. Camera repositioned lower and closer — looking down at the table surface. Locked camera.

SUBJECT: Both hands visible in frame — one holding a pen, writing on the open sketchbook. The mood board fills the frame: handwritten brand name sketches visible on the page, dark fabric swatches beside the book, torn editorial references. The sketchbook page is the hero of the shot.

STYLE ANCHOR: Same warm amber color grade. Key light from left window raking across the table surface — revealing texture of paper and fabric swatches. Shallow depth of field — pen tip and sketchbook page sharp, surroundings soft. No face visible in this shot.

ACTION: The pen moves slowly and deliberately across the page — writing or sketching a brand element. The motion is precise and unhurried. The fabric swatches shift very slightly as the hand brushes past them. Scene is intimate and focused — the act of creation in close detail.

---

LOGIC RULE: Each shot is camera-locked with no movement within the shot. The cut between Shot 1 and Shot 2 is clean — same environment, same lighting, same subject, different angle. No smile, no looking at camera, no sudden gestures at any point across both shots.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no camera movement within shots, no handheld shake, no smile, no eye contact with camera, no bright background, no abrupt motion, no inconsistent lighting between shots.
```

## Scena E — AI Tools + Voiceover (multi-shot)

**Durata: 6 secondi (3s + 3s)**
**Reference image Shot 1:** E1 (spalle alla camera)
**Reference image Shot 2:** E2 (3/4 frontale)
**Uso nel montaggio:** finale — "Just AI tools and an idea... You'll see what actually works. And what doesn't."
**Nota:** per Shot 2 attivare **Lip Sync** in Kling 3.0 con testo: *"You'll see what actually works. And what doesn't."*

```
FORMAT: Multi-shot cinematic sequence. Two shots. Single subject. Total duration: 6 seconds. Dark-luxury fashion atelier throughout. Fast cut between shots.

---

[SHOT 1 — 3 seconds]
Back shot, camera directly behind subject. Locked camera.

SUBJECT: Young man. Dark wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace. Seated at wooden work table, back fully to the camera — body and head squared toward the laptop, not turning. Back of head and shoulders in frame. Both hands on keyboard, typing actively. Face not visible.

ENVIRONMENT: Vintage dark-luxury atelier. Laptop open in front: multiple AI tool interfaces, dark mode, several windows open. Cool blue-white screen glow on his back and shoulders. Clothing rack with dark garments in background. Large window to the left, warm amber light. Warm golden undertones throughout.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic editorial fashion film. Warm amber color grade, deep shadows, golden-brown midtones. Dual light: warm window from left + cool screen glow from front on shoulders. Shallow depth of field — subject and screen sharp, background soft.

LOGIC RULE: Camera locked — no movement. Head stays pointed straight at screen throughout — does not turn left, does not turn right. Motion only in the hands typing. Fast and deliberate pace.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no turning head, no face visible, no looking at camera, no camera movement, no handheld shake, no smile.

ACTION: Subject types rapidly on the keyboard — both hands moving across the keys with intensity and purpose. The laptop screen flickers slightly as windows switch. The screen glow pulses softly on his shoulders. Scene is fast, focused, energetic — someone deep in the work.

---

[SHOT 2 — 3 seconds]
Fast cut. Camera repositioned to 3/4 frontal angle. Locked camera.

SUBJECT: Same young man. Now viewed from slightly in front and to the left — face clearly visible in 3/4. One hand resting on the table edge. Head turned slightly toward camera. Eyes direct. Expression stoic — jaw set, mouth flat until he begins speaking.

ENVIRONMENT: Same desk, same laptop open beside him, same atelier. Warm amber light from left window. Cool screen glow on the left side of his face.

STYLE ANCHOR: Same photorealistic editorial fashion film. Warm amber color grade, deep shadows. Dual light: warm window + cool screen glow on face. Shallow depth of field — face sharp, background soft. Expression stoic throughout — jaw set, mouth flat, zero upward curve, no raised cheeks.

DELIVERY: He speaks directly and calmly — stoic, no warmth, no smile. He says: "You'll see what actually works. And what doesn't." Mouth moves naturally with the words. Jaw set between phrases. Eyes do not waver.

LOGIC RULE: Camera locked — no movement. Expression stays stoic at all times — jaw set, mouth flat when not speaking, natural lip movement only during the delivery. No smile, no softening, no nodding. Expression is STOIC and LOCKED — no pleasant expression at any point.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no smile, no pleasant expression, no nodding, no camera movement, no handheld shake, no sudden gestures, no warmth in expression.

ACTION: Subject sits still and composed. He delivers the line "You'll see what actually works. And what doesn't." in a calm, direct tone — one beat of silence before speaking, then the line delivered flat and certain. Eyes stay on camera throughout. No movement besides the mouth.
```
