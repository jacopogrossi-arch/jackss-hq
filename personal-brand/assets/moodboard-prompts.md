# Jago — Mood Board Prompts
**Tool:** HiggsField Seedance 2.0 (immagini fisse, no Soul ID)
**Formato:** 9:16 verticale oppure 1:1 per mood board
**Palette:** nero #111110, indaco #1C2845, oliva #2E3320, avorio #EDE8DC

Genera 6-8 immagini, poi assembla in Canva su sfondo nero.

---

## 1. Tessuto lana scura — dettaglio

```
Extreme close-up of dark charcoal wool fabric. The textile fills the entire frame — 
no subject, no background. Fabric grain and weave clearly visible, slightly raised 
surface texture. Warm side light raking across the surface from the left, creating 
subtle shadows in the weave. Deep black and dark charcoal tones with warm amber 
undertones in the highlights.

Photorealistic macro fashion photography. Color grade: deep shadows, warm golden 
highlights, no cool tones. Film grain. No people, no hands, fabric only.
```

---

## 2. Denim selvage — dettaglio orlo

```
Extreme close-up of raw selvedge denim fabric. Focus on the selvedge edge — 
the woven border visible on one side, white with a thin indigo thread running 
through it. Deep indigo blue fabric, visible diagonal twill weave, slight fade 
at the edge. Warm raking light from the left. Dark studio background behind.

Photorealistic macro fashion photography. Color grade: deep indigo blues, warm 
amber light grazing the surface. Film grain visible. No people, fabric detail only.
```

## 3. Ricamo su tessuto nero — dettaglio logo

```

the thread, creating subtle highlights. The fabric texture is visible around the

Photorealistic macro fashion photography. Color grade: deep black fabric, warm ivory
```

## 4. Mani e tessuto — gesto artigianale

```
Close-up of a craftsman's hands holding dark fabric — examining the material, 
fingers slightly spread, fabric draped between them. Hands in the center of frame, 
fabric falling naturally. Warm amber light from the upper left. Dark studio 
background. No face visible, hands and fabric only.

Photorealistic editorial fashion photography. Color grade: warm amber tones, deep 
shadows, golden-brown midtones. Shallow depth of field. Film grain. Italian artisan 
quality — hands that know fabric.
```

## 5. Tavolo da lavoro — atelier

```
Wide shot of a vintage wooden work table in a fashion atelier. Spread across the 
surface: dark fabric swatches in black, charcoal, deep indigo and dark olive, 
a leather-bound sketchbook open with handwritten notes, a wooden ruler, chalk 
marks on dark fabric, metal scissors. Warm amber light from a large window on 
the left. Deep shadows on the right.

Photorealistic editorial photography. Color grade: warm amber tones, rich shadows, 
golden midtones. Overhead or slightly elevated angle. No people. Objects only. 
Film grain. Italian fashion atelier atmosphere.
```

```
slightly irregular — wax finish catches the light unevenly, creating a
```

## 7. Luce Toscana — ambiente esterno

```
Empty narrow street in a small Tuscan town, late afternoon golden hour. 
Stone walls in warm amber and ochre tones. Long shadows cast across the 
cobblestone ground. No people. The alley curves gently out of frame. 
A dark wooden door on the right side. Clothes drying lines barely visible 
above, casting a thin shadow.

Photorealistic architectural photography. Color grade: warm golden amber, 
deep shadows in the alley corners, high contrast between lit and shadowed 
surfaces. Film grain. Quiet, timeless, Italian.
```
