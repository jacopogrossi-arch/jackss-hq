## THE BRAND

**SCIROCCO** — *sun-bleached linen, in Mediterranean heat.*

A Sicilian resort label built around one fluid linen silhouette. No logos, no excess. The campaign has to feel sun-scorched, effortless, and expensive — the fabric moves with the wind, the light burns through it.

**One-line mood:** *He stands in the heat. The linen breathes. The sun does the work.*

---

## THE SIX PILLARS (locked before any tool opens)

### 1. Hero piece — ONE garment
**Fluid open linen shirt + wide-leg sand trousers.**
- Shirt: **raw off-white linen**, oversized fluid cut, **open chest**, loose-hanging — the fabric catches wind and light
- Trousers: **sand/ocra wide-leg**, high-waist, relaxed straight leg, **ankle length**, raw linen texture
- Worn together: shirt open over a **minimal fitted black tank** underneath
- The silhouette reads **relaxed but architectural** — not beach casual, not streetwear

### 2. World — ONE setting
**Sicilian coast, raw volcanic register.**
- **Black basalt lava rock**, whitewashed lime-plaster walls with geometric shadows, dammuso architecture, deep blue Mediterranean sea
- **Raw and ancient** — NOT polished resort, NOT tourist Amalfi. Pantelleria/Trapani register.
- One continuous Sicilian world across all five shots.

### 3. Light — ONE condition
**Hard overhead Mediterranean sun, 12:00–14:00.**
- Zenithal, intense, white-hot light — pure intensity, no warmth
- Short crisp shadows (sotto il mento, sotto le braccia, underfoot)
- The harshness is deliberate — hard light on soft linen is the visual tension
- Same condition in every shot.

### 4. Palette — 4 colors
**Bianco ottico + sabbia/ocra + basalto nero + blu mare profondo.**
- Garment: off-white linen shirt, sand-ocra trousers
- Environment: black lava rock, white lime plaster, deep blue sea/sky
- Nothing else enters the frame.

### 5. Model — one consistent character
**Jago.** Generated with **Nano Banana Pro + character sheet** (`personal-brand/assets/jago-character-sheet.jpeg`) — **NOT the Soul ID**. Same face, same identity across all five shots.

### 6. Mood — one sentence
*"Sun-bleached, still, volcanic — he doesn't move to the heat, he belongs to it."*
