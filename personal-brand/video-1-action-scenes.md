## Scena A — Macchina da cucire (0-3s, opening)

**Uso nel montaggio:** prima clip del video, sovrapposta al talking head Shot 1.
**Durata target:** 3-4s
**Reference image:** opzionale — puoi usare l'immagine atelier Seedream 5.0 come reference ambiente

```
FORMAT: Cinematic action scene. Single subject. Medium shot, slightly overhead angle, locked frame. Short clip — 3 to 4 seconds.

SUBJECT: Young man. Dark wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace with pendant. Seated at a sewing machine. Hands guiding fabric through the presser foot — deliberate, skilled, focused. Expression stoic and concentrated.

ENVIRONMENT: Modern dark-luxury atelier. Sewing machine in foreground. Metal shelving in background. Large window with diffused natural light from the side. Deep controlled shadows. Clean minimal workspace. Fabric scraps and pattern pieces on the table.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic editorial fashion film. Color grade: deep shadows, desaturated midtones, slight cool-warm contrast. Single key light from above-left, shallow depth of field on the hands and machine. No lens flare. No atmospheric haze. No motion blur.

LOGIC RULE: Camera is locked — no push-in, no pan, no movement. The action is in the hands and fabric only. Subject does not look up at camera. Expression remains focused and still.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no smile, no pleasant expression, no looking at camera, no handheld shake, no camera movement, no warmth, no softening, no bright lighting, no colorful environment.

ACTION: Subject guides dark fabric through the sewing machine — smooth, controlled motion of the hands. The needle moves rhythmically. Subject's gaze is fixed on the fabric, expression concentrated and stoic. Camera remains locked overhead-medium. The scene is quiet, deliberate, purposeful.
```

**Durata target:** 5-6s


## Scena C — Sfoglia tessuti (4-25s, mid-section)

**Uso nel montaggio:** voiceover sopra, nessun dialogo. Clip da 5-6s, puoi generarne 2 varianti (una mani-close-up, una medium shot).
**Durata target:** 5-6s

```
FORMAT: Cinematic action scene. Single subject. Medium shot or medium close-up on hands and upper body, locked frame. Short clip — 5 to 6 seconds.

SUBJECT: Young man. Dark wavy hair. Black t-shirt. Silver necklace with pendant. Standing or seated at a work table. Hands handling fabric swatches — lifting, feeling the texture, comparing two swatches. Expression stoic and evaluative. Not looking at camera.

ENVIRONMENT: Modern dark-luxury atelier. Work table with multiple fabric swatches spread out — dark colors (black, charcoal, deep navy, dark wool). Metal shelving background. Large window with diffused side light catching the texture of the fabrics. Deep controlled shadows. Minimal and purposeful workspace.

STYLE ANCHOR: Photorealistic editorial fashion film. Color grade: deep shadows, desaturated midtones, cool-warm contrast. The texture of the fabric is sharp and tactile — the key light rakes across the swatches to emphasize material quality. Shallow depth of field — hands and fabric sharp, background soft. No lens flare. No atmospheric haze.

LOGIC RULE: Camera is locked — no movement. Motion is entirely in the hands examining the fabric. Subject does not look at camera. Expression evaluative and still throughout.

NEGATIVE PROMPT: no smile, no pleasant expression, no looking at camera, no camera movement, no handheld shake, no bright colors, no colorful fabric, no warmth, no softening.

ACTION: Subject picks up a dark fabric swatch, holds it up slightly toward the side light to examine the texture, then places it back and picks up another to compare. Motion is slow and deliberate — the gesture of someone who understands material. Expression stoic and concentrated. Camera locked, scene quiet and purposeful.
```
