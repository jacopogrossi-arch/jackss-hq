ACTION: Subject speaks "What if AI could build a fashion brand?" — lips move cleanly, each word deliberate and even. Expression stoic and still throughout — jaw set, mouth flat, eyes direct, zero warmth. The face does not shift from this register before, during, or after the line. Slow smooth cinematic push-in across the entire shot — from medium close-up to tighter medium close-up. Stable camera, no shake, no handheld.

DELIVERY: Lips articulate "Follow if you want to see where this goes" — clean, measured, unhurried. Flat delivery throughout — no warmth on "follow," no upward inflection on "this goes." Spoken like a statement, not an invitation. Each word lands with equal weight.

LOGIC RULE: Expression is STOIC and LOCKED throughout — jaw set, mouth flat, no smile at any point, no softening, no pleasant expression. Camera position stays offset to the subject's left — no drift back to center. Push-in is slow, smooth, stable throughout the shot.

Slow smooth cinematic push-in from left-offset medium close-up to tighter left-offset medium close-up. Stable camera motion, no shake, no handheld.
